CONTAINERS

When it comes to eco-friendly containers, plastic containers are a poor option. Plastic is not biodegradable, resulting in it taking up space and polluting the environment. Plastic also contains toxic chemicals that harm both humans and wildlife. However, if no other option is available, you can make an effort to reuse the plastic containers.


Glass containers are less harmful to the environment since they are recyclable. Anchor and SKS make glass containers good for storage. They are also easy to wash and reuse. Stainless steel is also a good choice because it is durable, recyclable, and reusable. Mecete and Cal Tiffin are some businesses that offer eco-friendly stainless steel containers.
