ABOUT

Sustainable Alternatives is a website here to provide just that! Simple and alternative ways to practice sustainability and other ethical practices. We understand that many people feel an urge to help the environment but do not know where to start. Many have been made to think that only big and huge actions can make a change when the small changes can make just as much of a difference with the participation of enough people. Making even the minute changes in your day to day life can help make a difference for the betterment of the people and our beautiful planet Earth!
