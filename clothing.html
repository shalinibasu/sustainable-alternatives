SUSTAINABLE ALTERNATIVES

CLOTHING

Clothing is a product that nearly everyone invests in. However, most people are not educated on where their clothes come from. Clothes bought from fast fashion industries, which is where the majority of people shop, are produced unethically and unsustainably. Typically, the purpose of fast fashion companies is to be able to sell clothing that mimics that of high fashion clothing and trends while providing the consumer with cheap costs. Due to the fact that the clothing is incredibly inexpensive, two things are sacrificed: the quality and the wages of the laborers. The quality of clothing constantly declining makes it even more wasteful as the clothes cannot last as long. On top of this, fast fashion companies exploit women and children in developing world countries for their labor and take advantage of the lack of labor laws in these countries.
